Business Information

Site Purpose

The mission of Bountiful Foods is to create an ideal environment where people can enjoy local organic food alone, with friends or family. This goal is intended to be achieved through:

  • Fruits, vegetables and other types of food grown locally or purchased from the best suppliers.
  • Meals rich in vitamins, proteins and pleasant for those who love delicious and healthy food.
  • A pleasant environment where customers can find peace to clear all kinds of worries.

Bountiful Foods wants to become the preferred place when it comes to tasting delicious and organic food.s

Target Audience

Healthy people or people who wish to be healthy between 20 and 50 years old approximately. Some of these people are:

  • Fitness people who love exercise and eating healthy organic food.
  • Overweight people who want to improve their diet without stopping tasting delicious food.
  • People who like to rest from a busy day in a place that transmits tranquility, comfort, and offers delicious food.
  • Vegans.
